Shiyu Duan

Remaining image, 2021, pencil/paper/eraser leftovers, size variable.

In the process of continuous renewal and expansion, waste is its inevitable co-existence. Some ambiguous edge areas are formed by the stack of waste. The work shows the eraser waste generated by the pencil painting after being wiped, while retaining the original traces of the painting. The combination of the two is to capture the eternity of elapse in a fading dynamic.
