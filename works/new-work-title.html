Shiyu Duan

Chessboards, 2021, installation, variable size.

A chessboard is an item that accommodates chess pieces to indicate their position and status, which is a plane with three-dimensional space meanings. People often compare the chessboard to a battlefield, a field object. Different looking of chess pieces are always used as a metaphor for people of different classes, and at the same time people's responsibilities are associated with the visual characteristics of the chess pieces. The chess is divided into territory, elimination, race, stalemate, checkmate, formation, etc., each chess has their own rules, and the movement of chess pieces in a prescribed area is a game between regimes and classes. The artist borrows the form of chess pieces and chessboards, while removing the implicit hierarchy of the chess pieces, giving each piece a different shape to present a curious visual system.
